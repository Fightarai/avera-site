What is Avera?

Avera is an AI-driven engine for automated trading, combining classic quant strategies with Large Language Model (LLM) analysis for market bias and smarter execution.

Core Features

  • Adaptive quant strategies (RSI, VWAP, mean reversion)
  • LLM-based signal adjustment for market sentiment
  • Risk management layer and trade execution engine
  • Optional integration with dashboards and analytics

Sample Strategies

Examples: RSI with LLM bias overlay, VWAP-based trend following, mean reversion with macro bias adjustment.

LLM Integration

Our plugin uses GPT/FinGPT or similar LLMs to evaluate news sentiment, social media signals, or custom macro analysis, providing an extra layer on top of traditional trading rules.
